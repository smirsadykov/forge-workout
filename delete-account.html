Delete your account & data

App: app.forge.workout

You can permanently delete your FORGE account and all data associated with it. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open FORGE and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account, profile, and any synced workout data are deleted immediately and irreversibly.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can’t access the app, email us from the address tied to your account and we will delete it within 30 days. Put “Delete my account” in the subject.

Email a deletion request

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login (email + authentication record).
  • All workout data synced to the cloud (history, logs, settings, progression data).
  • Your subscription entitlement record.

What we may retain: limited purchase/transaction records that we are legally required to keep for tax and accounting. These contain no workout data.

Note: data stored only on your phone is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage. Cancelling a subscription is separate — manage that in Google Play.
