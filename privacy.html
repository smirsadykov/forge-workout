Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 June 2026 · Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the FORGE — Workout Generator application and website (collectively, the “App”) collect, use, and protect your information. The App is operated by the FORGE developer (“we”, “us”). By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Summary

  • We collect the minimum needed to run the App: your account email and the workout data you create.
  • We do not show ads, and we do not sell your data or use third-party advertising trackers.
  • Your workout history stays on your device unless you turn on cloud sync (a Premium feature).
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, from inside the App or via the web link below.

2. Information we collect

CategoryWhatWhy
Account Email address and an authentication token. To create your account, sign you in, and (for Premium) sync your data across devices.
Workout data Workouts you generate and log: exercises, sets, reps, weights, session settings, dates, and notes. To save your history, drive progression suggestions, and (for Premium) sync it.
Purchase status Whether you hold an active subscription or lifetime purchase, and the product purchased. Handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. To unlock Premium features. We never receive or store your card or payment details.
Device / technical Basic data needed for the app to function (e.g. app version, an anonymous install identifier used for purchases). To deliver features, validate purchases, and fix crashes.

We do not collect your name, precise location, contacts, photos, health-sensor data, or browsing activity outside the App.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the core features: generating and logging workouts and showing your progress.
  • To sync your data across your devices when you enable cloud sync (Premium).
  • To process and restore in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and diagnose technical problems.

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling, and we do not perform automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and synced data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and synced workout data are deleted (see Section 7). Local data on your device is removed when you uninstall the App or clear its storage. We may retain limited purchase records where required for tax, accounting, or legal compliance.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”);
  • Export your data in a portable format;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise any of these, use the in-app controls or contact us at the email below.

7. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the App: Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, your synced workout data, and your profile.
  • On the web: visit forge-workout / delete-account and follow the instructions, or email us a deletion request.

Account deletion is irreversible. Locally stored data is also cleared when you uninstall the App.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored by our providers with access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.
