Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-30

1. Who runs this app

FORGE (the "app") is an independent project run by Sergey Mirsadykov ("we", "us"). Contact: smirsadykov@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what the app needs to work for you:

  • Email and password. Used to create your account and log you in. Passwords are stored hashed by our auth provider — we never see them in plain text.
  • Workout data. The workouts you generate, save, and log: exercises, sets, reps, weights, dates, notes, soreness ratings, and saved templates.
  • Preferences. Units (kg/lb), language, theme, equipment you own, target rep ranges.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active FORGE Pro subscription (free / trial / pro), the product you subscribed to, and the renewal date. Used to unlock paid features. The actual payment is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play — we never see your card details. Subscription state is synced to our backend via the App Store / Play Store and our payment processor (RevenueCat) so it works across your devices.
  • Crash and error reports. When something breaks in the app we send a small diagnostic message (which screen you were on, what action triggered it, an error code). No screen contents, no personal data.

We do not collect: location, contacts, camera or microphone, advertising IDs, device IDs, payment card numbers, or any analytics for marketing or profiling.

3. Where it's stored

Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL hosted in AWS data centers). All traffic is encrypted with HTTPS. The app code itself is hosted on GitHub Pages. Subscription state is also stored by Apple, Google, and our payment processor RevenueCat (a US company that handles cross-platform subscription syncing) — strictly to keep your subscription working across devices.

4. Who can access it

Only you, when you're logged in. We don't sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. We don't run analytics on it. We don't use it to train any model.

The only third parties involved are technical service providers (Supabase for storage and auth, GitHub for hosting) — they handle the data only to keep the service running.

5. How long we keep it

We keep your data as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (or email us a deletion request at smirsadykov@gmail.com), we permanently remove all your records within 30 days.

Crash reports are kept for up to 90 days to debug recurring issues, then deleted.

6. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • See everything we store about you (the app shows it — your workouts, history, prefs).
  • Export your data — email us and we'll send a JSON export.
  • Correct your data — edit it in the app, or email us.
  • Delete your account and data — Settings → Delete account, or email us.
  • Withdraw consent — by deleting your account.

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you also have specific rights under GDPR, UK-GDPR, and CCPA. Reach out to the contact email above to exercise any of them — we respond within 30 days.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has registered, email us and we'll remove the account.

8. Health data disclaimer

The app stores workout and basic self-reported soreness/sleep ratings. This information is not classified as medical data and is not shared with anyone. Always consult a medical professional before starting any new exercise program.

9. Changes

We'll update this page when we change practices. The "Effective date" at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use after a material change means you accept it.

10. Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: smirsadykov@gmail.com.
